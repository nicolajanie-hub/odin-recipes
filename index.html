Odin Recipes

A collection of recipes that I like and have memorized. Most of them are simple at their core, with optional steps or additions that you can combine and experiment with, in addition to many steps having multiple ways they can be achieved. This is because I have health problems that affect my levels of energy day-to-day, so I often have to find workarounds or ways to conserve the energy I have.

If you look at something and think it's a "lazy option", it probably exists for the days I can't afford to stand in front of the oven, or do something with too many additional steps. But that doesn't mean that you have to be in poor health like me to benefit from these shortcuts, or that I'll judge you for doing so. Who knows? Maybe I'll be able to teach you something you'll benefit from next time you have the kind of day at work that's so taxing you're questioning if you'll have the energy to even drag yourself to bed.
